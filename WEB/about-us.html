The Minds Behind BirdBase

BirdBase is a visionary project brought to life by two young engineering students with a dream of uniting the unique beauties of nature with modern technology.

Proudly Supported by TÜBİTAK 2209-A

On this journey of innovation, the duo proved the potential of their idea and earned official backing under the TÜBİTAK 2209-A University Students Research Projects Support Program. With the support of science and national research institutions behind them, these two young engineers continue their work without slowing down, striving to turn their idea from a mere project into a flawless reality available for everyone.

Furkan Tuç

A senior engineering student who develops dynamic games in the hyper and hybrid casual genres within the mobile gaming industry. Specializing in game engines like Unity and Unreal Engine, as well as programming languages such as C++, C#, Java, and Python. Furkan focuses on how artificial intelligence and image processing technologies can be integrated to solve everyday problems. He leads the BirdBase project to use his technical expertise for the benefit of people and nature.

Nisa Doğa Yücel

A senior Computer Engineering student determinedly advancing towards becoming a senior Data Scientist and Machine Learning expert. She blends her engineering skills in C++ and Python with modern relational database management systems (RDBMS) and data mining principles. Doğa has made vital contributions to the project by rolling up her sleeves to flawlessly construct the massive dataset at the heart of BirdBase and personally train the advanced AI object detection models.
